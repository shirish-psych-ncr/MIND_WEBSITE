What happens in the first consultation?

Your first visit includes a detailed discussion of your concerns, medical history, and goals. Dr. Sharma will explain findings in plain language and collaborate with you on a treatment plan.

We provide documentation that you can submit for insurance reimbursement. Please check with your provider about mental health coverage.

Is the clinic child-friendly?

Yes. Our space is designed to be calm and welcoming for children. We specialize in developmental assessments and parent guidance.
