How this fits into the Indian system

RBSK (Government screening)

India’s national program screens for: Defects, Deficiencies, Diseases, and Developmental delays (4Ds).

If your child was flagged in school or anganwadi screening, we can help with further evaluation and therapy.

Learn about RBSK

DEIC referrals

Children identified through government programs are often referred to District Early Intervention Centres (DEIC).

We support ongoing therapy and follow-up after initial screening.

ASHA & early support

ASHA workers and MCP cards track early child development.

We help parents interpret these indicators and decide next steps.

Your child’s rights (India)

Disability certification & UDID

The UDID card allows access to government benefits, schemes, and support services.

Apply for UDID

Inclusive education

Under the RPWD Act (2016), children have the right to inclusive education, accommodations, and support in school.

View RPWD Act

Government support schemes

ADIP Scheme

Financial support for assistive devices such as hearing aids, mobility aids, and other equipment.

Niramaya Health Insurance

Affordable health insurance (up to ₹1 lakh) for children with developmental conditions.

Education support (SSA)

Ensures access to schooling and inclusive education for children with disabilities.
