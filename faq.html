Frequently asked questions before a first visit

Common questions about confidentiality, treatment approach, costs, and what to expect during your appointment at Mind Grace Neuropsychiatric Clinic.

Confidentiality

Yes. All information shared during consultations is strictly confidential and used only for your care and appointment coordination. The only exceptions are rare situations where there is an immediate risk to your safety or others', where we may need to contact emergency services or relevant authorities.

Only Dr. Anita Sharma and essential administrative staff involved in scheduling and billing have access to your records. We do not share information with third parties without your explicit written consent, except as required by law.

Medication and Treatment

Not necessarily. Medication is considered when clinically appropriate, but we also offer practical strategies, behavioural interventions, and therapeutic options. The treatment plan is personalized based on your specific needs, preferences, and clinical assessment.

We offer both medication management and psychotherapy services. Depending on your needs, we may recommend individual therapy, family counseling, cognitive-behavioural techniques, or a combination of approaches alongside any medication if prescribed.

Booking and Logistics

You can book an appointment by filling out our online booking form, calling us directly at +91 96678 63295, or sending a WhatsApp message. We'll confirm your appointment within 24–48 hours via your preferred contact method.

Initial psychiatric consultations typically range from ₹1500–₹2500 depending on the complexity and duration. Follow-up sessions and therapy have different rates. Please see our fees page for detailed pricing information.

We accept most major health insurance plans that cover mental health services. Please check with your insurance provider about coverage for psychiatric consultations and therapy. We can provide receipts and documentation for insurance claims.
