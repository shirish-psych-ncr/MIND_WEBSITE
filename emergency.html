You’re not alone — but this needs immediate support

If you or someone near you is in immediate danger, at risk of self-harm, experiencing a severe mental health crisis, or has taken an overdose — please act now.

Call emergency services (112) or go to the nearest hospital immediately.

What you can do right now

  • Stay with the person — do not leave them alone
  • Remove access to anything harmful (medications, sharp objects, etc.)
  • Speak calmly — you don’t need the perfect words, just be present
  • Reach out to emergency services or a helpline immediately

24/7 Helplines (India)

  • Kiran Mental Health Helpline: 1800-599-0019
  • Vandrevala Foundation: 9999 666 555
  • Childline: 1098 (for children in distress or harm)

If one line is busy, try another. Keep trying.

This page is for urgent situations
