Overthinking vs anxiety

Overthinking can be a habit, but it can also be a sign that the nervous system is staying stuck in anxious alert mode.

Quick distinction

Overthinking is often the mental pattern. Anxiety is the wider state that may also affect your body, sleep, and sense of safety.
