Speech delay red flags parents should notice

Parents often notice a delay before anyone else does. Early observation can make the next steps much clearer.

Look for patterns, not one isolated moment

  • Limited words compared with age expectations
  • Difficulty understanding simple instructions
  • Frustration because communication is hard
