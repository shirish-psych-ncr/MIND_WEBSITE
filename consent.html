Consent Notice

By submitting an enquiry or appointment request, you consent to Dr. Anita Sharma clinic using your contact information for appointment coordination and care-related communication.

Information We Collect

When you submit an enquiry or appointment request through our website, we collect the following information:

  • Your name and contact details (phone number, email address)
  • Preferred appointment date and time
  • Brief description of your concern (optional)
  • Any specific doctor preference

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for:

  • Scheduling and confirming appointments
  • Communicating about your care
  • Sending appointment reminders
  • Responding to your enquiries

Your Consent

By submitting your information through this website, you explicitly consent to:

  • Dr. Anita Sharma and Mind Grace Clinic staff using your contact information for appointment coordination
  • Receiving phone calls, SMS, or emails related to your appointment
  • Secure storage of your information in our patient management system

Confidentiality Assurance

All patient information is treated with strict confidentiality in accordance with:

  • The Mental Healthcare Act, 2017
  • RCI (Rehabilitation Council of India) guidelines
  • Medical council ethical guidelines

Website Content Disclaimer

The content on this website is for educational and informational purposes only. It is not a substitute for:

  • Professional medical diagnosis
  • Personalized treatment recommendations
  • Emergency medical care

Always consult with a qualified healthcare provider for any health concerns.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information held by the clinic
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent for non-essential communications
  • Request deletion of your data subject to legal requirements
