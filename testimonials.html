What to expect (honest clarity)

Mental health treatment is not instant or identical for everyone.

  • Some patients improve quickly, others require longer follow-up
  • Medication response varies from person to person
  • Therapy and lifestyle changes are often essential alongside medication
  • Consistency matters more than quick fixes

If something is not working, treatment is adjusted — not abandoned.

We also take feedback seriously

A small number of reviews mention concerns around waiting time or staff communication.

We actively work on improving clinic coordination and ensuring a respectful experience at every step.

If you ever face an issue, you can directly reach out — we take it seriously.
