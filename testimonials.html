Honest clarity about treatment

Mental health care is not one-size-fits-all. Here's what to keep in mind:

  • Progress takes time: Improvement often happens gradually over weeks or months, not overnight.
  • Medication may need adjustment: Finding the right medication and dose can require patience and follow-up visits.
  • Therapy requires participation: The more actively you engage in recommended strategies, the better the outcomes tend to be.
  • Setbacks are normal: Recovery isn't linear. Bad days don't mean treatment isn't working.

Feedback we're working on

We take all patient feedback seriously. Here are areas we're actively improving:

Waiting times

Some patients have mentioned longer-than-expected waits. We're implementing better scheduling systems to minimize delays while ensuring each patient receives adequate consultation time.

Communication between visits

We're exploring secure messaging options to make it easier for patients to reach out with non-urgent questions between appointments.
