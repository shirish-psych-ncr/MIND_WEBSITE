Conditions We Treat

Expert care for mental health conditions affecting adults and children, delivered by experienced psychiatrists and clinical psychologists.

Anxiety Disorders

Generalized Anxiety Disorder

Persistent worry, restlessness, muscle tension, and sleep disturbances affecting daily life.

  • Excessive worry
  • Restlessness
  • Sleep problems
Psychiatry Care Try Breathing Tool

Panic Disorder

Recurrent panic attacks with intense fear, heart palpitations, sweating, and shortness of breath.

  • Panic attacks
  • Heart palpitations
  • Fear of losing control
Psychiatry Care Learn More

Social Anxiety Disorder

Fear of social situations, judgment, or embarrassment leading to avoidance behaviors.

  • Fear of judgment
  • Social avoidance
  • Physical symptoms in crowds
Therapy Support Self-Help Guide

Mood Disorders

Depression

Persistent sadness, loss of interest, fatigue, and changes in sleep or appetite affecting quality of life.

  • Persistent sadness
  • Loss of interest
  • Fatigue
Psychiatry Care Psychotherapy

Bipolar Disorder

Mood episodes alternating between depression and mania or hypomania requiring specialized management.

  • Mood swings
  • Manic episodes
  • Depressive episodes

Dysthymia

Chronic low-grade depression lasting years, often overlooked but treatable with proper intervention.

  • Chronic sadness
  • Low energy
  • Low self-esteem
Treatment Options Understanding Dysthymia

Neurodevelopmental Conditions

Autism Spectrum Disorder

Differences in social communication, repetitive behaviors, and sensory processing. Early intervention improves outcomes.

  • Social communication differences
  • Repetitive behaviors
  • Sensory sensitivity

ADHD

Inattention, hyperactivity, and impulsivity affecting children and adults. Comprehensive assessment and treatment available.

  • Inattention
  • Hyperactivity
  • Impulsivity

Speech & Language Delay

Delayed speech development in children requiring early intervention and speech therapy support.

  • Late talking
  • Limited vocabulary
  • Difficulty forming sentences
Speech Therapy Parent Guide

Obsessive-Compulsive & Related Disorders

OCD

Unwanted intrusive thoughts (obsessions) and repetitive behaviors (compulsions) causing significant distress.

  • Intrusive thoughts
  • Compulsive behaviors
  • Time-consuming rituals
Psychiatry Care CBT/ERP Therapy

PTSD

Trauma-related symptoms including flashbacks, nightmares, avoidance, and hypervigilance after distressing events.

  • Flashbacks
  • Nightmares
  • Avoidance behaviors
Trauma Therapy Grounding Tool

Schizophrenia

Complex condition affecting thinking, perception, emotions, and behavior. Requires comprehensive psychiatric care.

  • Hallucinations
  • Delusions
  • Disorganized thinking

Schizoaffective Disorder

Combination of psychotic symptoms and mood episodes requiring integrated treatment approach.

  • Psychotic symptoms
  • Mood episodes
  • Functional impairment
