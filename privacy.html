Privacy Notice

Contact details shared through the website are used only for appointment coordination and care-related communication. The clinic collects only the minimum required information and maintains confidentiality for all patients.

Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our services:

  • Name and contact details (phone number, email address)
  • Appointment preferences (date, time, doctor preference)
  • Brief description of your concern (optional, for triage purposes)
  • Medical history relevant to your treatment (collected during consultation, not via website)

How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used exclusively for:

  • Scheduling and managing appointments
  • Providing healthcare services and treatment
  • Communicating about your care plan
  • Sending appointment reminders and follow-ups
  • Maintaining medical records as required by law

Data Protection & Confidentiality

We maintain strict confidentiality in accordance with:

  • The Mental Healthcare Act, 2017 - Section 21 (Right to Confidentiality)
  • RCI (Rehabilitation Council of India) Code of Ethics
  • Indian Medical Council (Professional Conduct) Regulations
  • Applicable data protection laws and regulations

Data Storage & Security

Your information is stored securely through:

  • Encrypted patient management systems
  • Password-protected electronic health records
  • Secure physical filing systems for paper records
  • Restricted access limited to authorized clinical staff

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by law or court order
  • For continuity of care with your explicit consent
  • In emergency situations where your safety is at risk
  • With other healthcare providers involved in your treatment (with your consent)

Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data subject to legal retention requirements
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request limitation on how we use your information
  • Right to Data Portability: Request transfer of your data to another provider

Website Data Collection

Our website may automatically collect:

  • Browser type and version
  • IP address (anonymized for analytics)
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referral source

This information is used only for website improvement and is not linked to your personal identity.

Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies for essential functionality. No tracking cookies or advertising pixels are used.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information:

  • For as long as you remain an active patient
  • For the period required by medical record retention laws (minimum 5 years after last consultation)
  • Until you request deletion, subject to legal obligations

Children's Privacy

For patients under 18 years of age:

  • Parental or guardian consent is required for treatment
  • Information is shared with parents/guardians as appropriate for the child's care
  • Adolescent confidentiality is respected according to clinical guidelines and legal requirements
