About Dr. Sharma

Dr. Anita Sharma is a Consultant Psychiatrist with over 15 years of experience in mental health care. She completed her medical training at King George's Medical College, Lucknow, followed by specialized psychiatry training at the Institute of Human Behaviour and Allied Sciences, Delhi.

Her expertise extends internationally, having trained at the prestigious Maudsley Hospital in London, where she gained valuable insights into cutting-edge psychiatric treatments and therapies. Dr. Sharma believes in a patient-centered approach, combining medication management with psychotherapy to provide comprehensive care tailored to each individual's needs.

At Mind Grace Neuropsychiatric Clinic, she leads a multidisciplinary team committed to reducing stigma and making mental healthcare accessible, confidential, and effective for all patients.
