Understanding Our Roles

How Dr. Anita (Psychiatrist) and Dr. Sana (Psychologist) Can Help You

Both professionals work together to provide integrated, holistic care.

🩺 Psychiatrist (Dr. Anita)

Medical Doctor (MD) specializing in mental health. Can prescribe medication and manage complex biological aspects of mental illness.

  • Diagnoses medical conditions
  • Prescribes & manages medication
  • Treats complex biological issues
  • Handles severe mental illness
  • Provides medical certificates
  • Manages hospital admissions

Best for: Medication needs, severe depression, bipolar disorder, schizophrenia, addiction medical management, complex cases requiring medical intervention.

🧠 Psychologist (Dr. Sana)

Clinical Psychologist (M.Phil) specializing in psychotherapy and psychological testing. Provides talk therapy and behavioral interventions.

  • Provides psychotherapy & counseling
  • Conducts psychological testing
  • Teaches coping skills & strategies
  • Child development & behavior
  • Trauma processing & talk therapy
  • Learning disability assessment

Best for: Talk therapy, anxiety management, child behavioral issues, learning disabilities, relationship counseling, trauma processing, skill-building.

🤝 Common Services (Both Specialists)

Many patients benefit from seeing both specialists for integrated, comprehensive care.
