About Our Clinic

A Clinical Approach Built Around
Clarity, Pace & Respect

Dr Anita Sharma works with adults, adolescents, children, and families who want a calmer, clearer first step into mental health or developmental care. The focus is not only on naming a concern, but on understanding what is happening in daily life and what kind of support is most likely to help.

Book a Consultation What to Expect

How Care Is Approached

Built on principles of understanding, clarity, and meaningful outcomes

Treatment Explained Plainly

Medication, therapy, parent guidance, and developmental pathways are discussed in understandable language.

Function Matters

Better sleep, steadier mood, improved concentration, school functioning, and family life are treated as real outcomes.

Training and Clinical Background

  • MD Psychiatry, Guru Gobind Singh Medical College and Hospital, Faridkot
  • MBBS, Universal College of Medical Sciences
  • Senior Residency at AIIMS Rishikesh and IHBAS Delhi
  • Clinical work spanning adult psychiatry, child development, parent guidance, and structured outpatient care

Clinic Environment

The clinic experience is designed to feel calm, direct, and easier to navigate for both adults and families.

Research and Professional Interests

  • Published work on family burden in substance dependence and primary care psychiatry proceedings
  • Participation in academic and continuing medical education forums including psychiatry and addiction-related programs
  • Clinical interest in practical outpatient care for anxiety, mood concerns, ADHD, development, and parent support
