Psychological Assessments & Testing

Comprehensive evaluations for accurate diagnosis

Assessment fees vary based on scope

Psychological testing is quoted after an initial screening to determine the appropriate assessment battery. Common assessments include IQ testing, ADHD evaluation, autism spectrum assessment, and personality inventories. Typical range: ₹2,000 – ₹8,000 depending on complexity.

Insurance & Reimbursement

Mental health coverage information

Mental Healthcare Act, 2017 Compliance

As per Indian law, mental health conditions must be covered by insurance providers just like physical health conditions. We provide detailed invoices and medical documentation to support your reimbursement claims.

Government Insurance

CGHS, ECHS, and other government schemes accepted with proper documentation.

Private Insurance

Major providers including Star Health, HDFC Ergo, ICICI Lombard, and more.

Corporate Plans

Many employer-provided health plans include mental health coverage. Check with HR.

Cancellation & Rescheduling Policy

We understand life happens — here's our flexible approach

24-Hour Notice Requested

To avoid charges, please cancel or reschedule at least 24 hours before your appointment. Late cancellations (less than 24 hours) may incur a 50% fee. No-shows will be charged the full consultation fee. We maintain this policy to respect the time of our clinicians and other patients waiting for appointments.
